About IIAC 2024

The International Industry Academia Conference(IIAC 2024) will be held in Hotel Singar International,Kanyakumari, India during December 28 & 29, 2024 in association with Institute of Engineers(IE), India and Institute of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers(IETE) with an objective to serve as a platform for scientists,

researchers, engineers, students and developers from a wide range of Industry and Academy areas to exchange ideas and applications.

The theme of this conference is to discuss about the emerging innovations and applications of science and engineering and also the role of digital marketing in our day to day life. There will be invited talks from industrial experts, Oral paper presentation and poster presentation sessions.This conference cordially invites submission of students and researches concerning any branch of Engineering and Management studies and the participation of anyone who are interested in the related fields.
